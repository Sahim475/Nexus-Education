What sets us apart

Our supply teacher agency offers unique features that differentiate us from our competitors. Here are some of the key reasons why we stand out:

We offer a personalized matching process that connects schools with the right supply teachers based on their specific requirements.

Rapid Response Time

We have a dedicated team of consultants available around the clock to help schools find supply teachers at short notice.

Extensive Database

Our agency has an extensive database of supply teachers with a wide range of skills and experience. We carefully vet all of our teachers to ensure that they meet our high standards, and we match them with schools based on their specific needs.

We understand that every school has unique requirements when it comes to finding the right supply teacher.

That's why we've developed a personalised matching process that takes into account a wide range of factors to connect schools with the perfect candidate.

Our process begins with a comprehensive consultation where we'll learn about your school's needs, preferences and any specific requirements you may have. We'll then use this information to search our extensive database of experienced and qualified supply teachers to find the perfect match for your school.

  • Schools can ensure supply staff sent to them have necessary skills and experience which can lead to improved teaching quality and student outcomes.
  • We will do the work of finding the most suitable candidates based on your requirements, saving you time and effort by not having to sift through numerous teacher profiles.
  • Schools can benefit from our personalised approach to matching supply teachers, as they will be valued and supported by us. This means better retention rates among supply staff, which reduces the need for frequent recruitment and training.
  • Our personalised matching process means schools receive a tailored solution that meets their needs rather than a one size fits all approach. This can help school to address unique challenges and achieve their goals more effectively.

Overall, our personalised matching process can provide schools with numerous benefits, including improved teacher quality, time savings, better retention rates, reduced costs, higher satisfaction rates, and tailored solutions.

We understand that every school has unique needs and preferences when it comes to hiring supply teachers.

That's why we go beyond basic information and qualifications in our teacher profiles, providing a comprehensive view of each candidate's skills, experience, and personality.

Our teacher profiles include:

  • Teaching specializations: We list the subjects and grade levels each teacher is qualified to teach, as well as any additional skills or certifications they have, such as experience with special needs students or fluency in multiple languages.
  • Work history: We provide a detailed overview of each teacher's work history, including where they've worked before and what their responsibilities were. This gives schools a better idea of what kind of experience each teacher brings to the table.
  • Teaching philosophy: We ask each teacher to share their teaching philosophy, so that schools can get a sense of their approach to education and whether it aligns with their own values.
  • We gather feedback from schools and other education providers where each teacher has worked previously, so that schools can get an idea of how well they've performed in the past.
  • Personal information: Finally, we include a section for each teacher to share some personal information about themselves, such as their hobbies and interests, to help schools get to know them on a more personal level.

Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our Frequently Asked Questions section! Here you'll find answers to some of the most commonly asked questions about our supply teacher agency and the unique features that set us apart.

  • How do I register with your supply teacher agency?

    You can register with our agency by completing the online registration form on our website which can be found by clicking on the Get Started button at the top. We will then review your application and contact you if we require any further information.

  • What qualifications do I need to become a supply teacher?

    You need to have a teaching qualification and be registered with the appropriate teaching regulatory body, such as the General Teaching Council for England (GTCE). You may also need to undergo a Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) check.

  • How do you match supply teachers to schools?

    We carefully match supply teachers to schools based on their qualifications, experience, and availability. We also take into consideration the specific needs and requirements of the school.

  • What types of schools do you provide supply teachers for?

    We provide supply teachers for a range of schools, including primary schools, secondary schools, and special educational needs (SEN) schools.

  • What support do you provide for your supply teachers?

    We provide ongoing support and guidance to our supply teachers, including access to training and professional development opportunities. We also have a dedicated team available to answer any questions or concerns you may have.
